Why Teams Use TraceLM

Built for the operational reality of production LLM systems

LLM features rarely fail in obvious ways. They drift. They slow down. Costs creep upward. A prompt that worked last week starts producing shorter answers, weaker structure, or increased fallback responses. TraceLM is designed to make those shifts visible with request-level detail, quality scoring, and a concise operations dashboard your team can actually use day to day.

For engineering teams

Monitor latency distributions, identify the slowest requests, compare model behavior, and quickly connect operational issues to actual traffic.

For platform and ops

Track spend, request throughput, outlier prompts, retention windows, and overall service health from a single control surface.

For product leaders

Measure whether the system is becoming faster, more expensive, or lower quality over time, without waiting for anecdotal feedback.

For responsible rollout

Compare prompt and model changes with confidence by keeping a historical view of request behavior, token usage, and quality signals.

How It Works

From raw LLM traffic to an operational control plane

1. Collect request traces

Send request metadata, token counts, prompts, responses, status, latency, and cost into the collector.

2. Score and enrich responses

TraceLM computes heuristic quality signals and organizes request data into actionable metrics and insights.

3. Monitor live behavior

Use the dashboard to detect regressions, inspect slow or expensive requests, and track quality over time.

What You Can Answer
